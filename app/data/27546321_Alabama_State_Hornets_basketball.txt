The Alabama State Hornets basketball team is the men's basketball team that represents Alabama State University in Montgomery, Alabama, United States. The school's team currently competes in the Southwestern Athletic Conference. ==AAMU basketball rivalry== The Alabama State-AAMU basketball rivalry is annually the highest attended and most anticipated series for both schools. ==Postseason results== In seven total postseason appearances the Hornets are yet to win a game. ===NCAA tournament results=== The Hornets have appeared in the NCAA tournament four times.
