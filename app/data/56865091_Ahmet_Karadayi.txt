Ahmet Karadayi (born 19 January 1996) is a Belgian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Turkish club Nazilli Belediyespor. ==Professional career== Born in Belgium and of Turkish descent, while his mother got Kurdish roots too. Karadayi begun playing football at his local club Weerstand Koersel at the age of 7, and worked his way to Sporting Hasselt in the 4th division in Belgium. He moved up a division, joining Geel in 2016. Karadayi moved from AS Geel in the Belgian First Amateur Division to Kardemir Karabükspor in the Süper Lig on 16 January 2018, after a couple successful seasons.
