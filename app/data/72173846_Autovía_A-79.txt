The Autovía A-79 is a local highway between Alicante and Elche, Spain. This pathway is usually known as Vía Parque Alicante-Elche. Its length is about 14 km. This road runs from the junction with Autovía A-31, near to Alicante city, towards the junction with Autovía EL-20, the access road to Elche. Officially, this route is divided into two parts.
