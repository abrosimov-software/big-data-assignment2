Amy Leach is an American non-fiction writer. She won a 2010 Whiting Award. She won a 2008 Rona Jaffe Foundation Writers' Award. ==Life== She graduated from the University of Iowa with an MFA in creative nonfiction. She teaches literature at the University of St. Francis.
