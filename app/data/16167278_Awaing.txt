Awaing is a village in Ye Township in the Mon State of south-east Burma. It is located approximately 14 kilometres north-east of Ye city. Nearby towns and villages include Sonmatha (2.2 nm), Sakale (2.2 nm), Kaleiktok (3.5 nm), Wekwa (3.9 nm), Pawtaw (2.2 nm) and Awagyaik (2.0 nm). ==References== Category:Populated places in Mon State
