thumb|Agartala Flyover The Agartala flyover is a flyover in the Indian state of Tripura. The flyover, the first in the state, is 2.26 km long, stretching from the police line (Drop Gate) in southern Agartala to Fire Brigade chowmuhuni (square) in the heart of the city. The Construction is carried out by NCC and it was opened in 2019.
