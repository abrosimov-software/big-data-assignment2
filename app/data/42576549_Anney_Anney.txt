Anney Anney () is a 1983 Indian Tamil-language film written and directed by Mouli. The film stars him, Viji and Sumithra. It was released on 7 July 1983, and was a hit at the box office. == Plot == == Cast == *Mouli as Krishnagiri *Sumithra as Durga Krishnagiri *Viji as Rathna * Vanitha Krishnachandran as Parvathy (Paaru) *Charle as Faffu *V.
