Agency Creek is a stream in the U.S. state of Idaho. It is a tributary of the Lemhi River. Agency Creek was named for an Indian agency near its course. ==Course== Agency Creek rises on the west side of Lemhi Pass and then flows westerly to join the Lemhi River at Tendoy, Idaho. ==Watershed== Agency Creek drains of area, receives about 20.2 in/year of precipitation, has a wetness index of 290.19, and is about 32% forested. ==References== Category:Rivers of Lemhi County, Idaho Category:Rivers of Idaho
