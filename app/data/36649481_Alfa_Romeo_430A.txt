The Alfa Romeo 430A was a bus produced from 1949 to 1953 by Italian automotive manufacturer Alfa Romeo. ==Technical characteristics== The bus had two doors and a rack. It had a engine. The bus had seating for 22 passenger and standing room for a further 30 people. The vehicle was in length. It was based on a body from Ambrosini. ==History== The Alfa Romeo 430A bus was used for tourist trips and for intercity service in the Lombardy region of Italy. ==See also== * Alfa Romeo 430 * List of buses 430A
