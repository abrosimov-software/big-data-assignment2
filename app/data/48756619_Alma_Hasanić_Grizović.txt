Alma Hasanić Grizović (born 13 May 1989) is a Bosnian-Norwegian retired handball player. Grizović was born in Tešanj, SR Bosnia and Herzegovina to Bosnian parents, and was raised in Norway. She made her debut on Norway women's national handball team on 8 June 2014, against South Korea. Because the goalkeeper competition was hard on the Norwegian team, she decided to try out for Montenegrin national team, as she had family who was Montenegrin.
