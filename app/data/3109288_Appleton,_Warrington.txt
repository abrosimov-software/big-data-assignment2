Appleton is a civil parish and suburb of Warrington, in the Warrington district, in the ceremonial county of Cheshire, England. ==Geography and landmarks== The A49 road runs from neighbouring Stockton Heath up the hill into Appleton and links Warrington town centre with the M56 motorway. At the top of Appleton is Warrington Golf Club. There is also a large cemetery situated nearby which is named Fox Covert Cemetery. The cemetery is locally called Hillcliffe.
