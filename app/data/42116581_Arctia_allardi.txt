Arctia allardi is a moth in the family Erebidae. It was described by Charles Oberthür in 1911. It is found in China (Sichuan, Qinghai, and eastern Tibet).Checklist of Arctiidae of the Palaearctic region The species of the genus Preparctia , including this one, were moved to Arctia as a result of phylogenetic research published by Rönkä et al. in 2016. ==Subspecies== *Arctia allardi allardi *Arctia allardi tibetica Dubatolov, Kishida & C.S.
