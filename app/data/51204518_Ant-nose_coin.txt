Ant-nose coin (, coin like the nose of ant), also called ant nose money,ant- nose money / coin yibi cowry, Yibi coin and so on, was a small bronze coin minted by the state of Chu during the Warring States period. In Chinese, it is also called "鬼脸钱" (guǐ liǎn qián, coin like the face of a ghost). == See also == * Zhou dynasty coinage == References == Category:Coins of ancient China Category:Bronze coins Category:Chu (state) Category:Chinese numismatics
