A. M. A. Hamid was a Member of the National Assembly of Pakistan representing East Bengal. == Career == Hamid was elected to the 1st National Assembly of Pakistan from East Pakistan as a Muslim candidate in 1947. Hamid spoke against the allegation of violence against Hindu minorities by congress members of Parliaments from East Pakistan.
