Antilhue II Power Plant is an oil-fueled electrical generating station in Antilhue, Los Ríos Region, Chile. The plant uses diesel as fuel and produces 51.6 MW of electricity.Electricity generation capacity of Chile by Comisión Nacional de Energía The plant was built in 2005 and is operated by Colbún S.A.Descripción de cada central by Colbún S.A. ==References== Category:Energy infrastructure in Los Ríos Region Category:Oil-fired power stations in Chile
