Aftab Hussain Siddiqui is a Pakistani politician who had been a member of the National Assembly of Pakistan from October 2018 till January 2023. He had secured a seat vacant by incumbent President of Pakistan, Arif Alvi. ==Political career== He was elected to the National Assembly of Pakistan from Constituency NA-247 (Karachi South-II) as a candidate of Pakistan Tehreek-e- Insaf in by-election held on 21 October 2018.
