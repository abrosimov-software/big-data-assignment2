Alvin Tehau (born April 10, 1989) is a Tahitian professional association footballer who plays for A.S. Tefana as a central midfielder. He is the twin brother of Lorenzo Tehau, brother of Jonathan Tehau and cousin of Teaonui Tehau, all playing for Tahiti national football team. Tehau is a full-time member of the Tahiti national football team, and represented his country at the 2013 FIFA Confederations Cup.
