Akuka Albert Alalzuuga is a Ghanaian politician and member of the Seventh Parliament of the Fourth Republic of Ghana representing the Garu Constituency in the Upper East Region on the ticket of the National Democratic Congress (NDC). == Early life and education == Akuka Albert Alalzuuga was born on 28 February 1968.
