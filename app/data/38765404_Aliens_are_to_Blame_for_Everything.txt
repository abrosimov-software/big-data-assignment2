Aliens are to Blame for Everything (, ) is a 1991 Yugoslav film by Zoran Čalić starring Bata Živojinović, Boro Stjepanović, Nikola Simić and others. The film is also known as Ćao inspektore, III deo ("Bye, Inspector, Part III"). ==Plot== The election campaign in a small provincial town is in full swing. Candidates for ministry position pop up elsewhere, promising "milk and honey" to the voters. Two inseparable policemen, Boki and Pajko, are in charge of peace and order.
