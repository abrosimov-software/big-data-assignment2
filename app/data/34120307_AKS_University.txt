AKS University (Amicable Knowledge Solution) is a private university in Satna, Madhya Pradesh, India. It is the one of the private universities in the region of Vindhya and Mahakoshal. It was established under M.P Legislature Act No.44 duly recognized under section 2(f) passed on 23 November 2011.
