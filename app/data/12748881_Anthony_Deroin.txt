Anthony Nicolas Michel Deroin (born 15 March 1979) is a French former professional footballer who played his entire career for Stade Malherbe Caen as a midfielder. ==Career== Deroin spent his whole career with Stade Malherbe Caen, from 1997 to 2012, scoring 35 goals in 368 appearances (9 goals in 102 in Ligue 1). He was promoted in French Ligue 1 with his club in 2004, 2007 and 2010. In 2008, he surpassed Yvan Lebourgeois's record of 320 appearances for Caen.
