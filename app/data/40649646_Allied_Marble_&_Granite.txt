Allied Marble & Granite Inc. is a private company based in Seattle, Washington known for its West Coast residential and commercial manufactured stone product installations. Thursday, 22 April 2021 ==History== Quang Mai is the owner of Allied Marble and Jimmy Mai serves as the company's executive vice president. The company was founded by Mai's father.
