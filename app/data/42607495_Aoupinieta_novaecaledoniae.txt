Aoupinieta novaecaledoniae is a species of moth of the family Tortricidae. It was described by Razowski in 2012 and is endemic to New Caledonia. The wingspan is about . The ground colour of the forewings is yellow with dark orange reticulation and dark brown markings. The hindwings are creamish. ==Etymology== The species name refers to the country of origin., 2012: Five tortricines from Malaysia and New Caledonia (Lepidoptera: Tortricidae). Polish Journal of Entomology 81 (1): 81-90.
