Abhimanyusingh Rajput (born 9 May 1998) is an Indian cricketer. He made his List A debut on 7 October 2019, for Baroda in the 2019–20 Vijay Hazare Trophy. He made his first-class debut on 9 December 2019, for Baroda in the 2019–20 Ranji Trophy.
