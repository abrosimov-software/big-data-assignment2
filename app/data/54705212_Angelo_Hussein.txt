Hussain Angelo Koko Nimer (Arabic: حسين أنجلو كوكو نمر) was a Sudanese middle- distance track and field athlet. He competed in the 400 m, 800 m and 4 × 400 m events at the 1968 and 1972 Summer Olympics, but was unfortunate to reach the finals. ==References== 2\.
