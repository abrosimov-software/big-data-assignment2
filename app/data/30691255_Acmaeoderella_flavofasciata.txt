Acmaeoderella flavofasciata is a species of jewel beetles belonging to the family Buprestidae, subfamily Polycestinae. This beetle is present in most of Europe, in the eastern Palearctic realm, and in North Africa. The adults are long. Head and pronotum are wrinkled and hairy.
