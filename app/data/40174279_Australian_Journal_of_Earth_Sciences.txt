The Australian Journal of Earth Sciences is a peer-reviewed scientific journal that is published eight times per year by Taylor & Francis on behalf of the Geological Society of Australia. The journal broadly covers the earth sciences. The editor-in-Chief is A.S. Andrew (New South Wales).
