Aleksandar Milenković (, born 22 December 1967) is a Serbian cyclist, biathlete and cross-country skier. Milenković has competed at two Winter and one Summer Olympic Games representing Yugoslavia, Independent Olympic Participants and Serbia and Montenegro. == Olympic career == === 1992 Winter Olympics === At the 1992 Winter Olympics, Milenković represented Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia and competed in cross-country skiing. * 10 kilometres - 87th * 30 kilometres - 80th * 50 kilometres - 65th * 10/15 kilometres Pursuit - 75th === 1992 Summer Olympics === At the 1992 Summer Olympics, Milenković competed as Independent Olympic Participant in cycling. * Road Race - 42nd * 100 kilometres Team Time Trial - 18th === 2006 Winter Olympics === At the 2006 Winter Olympics, Milenković represented Serbia and Montenegro and competed in biathlon and cross-country skiing. ==== Biathlon ==== * 10 kilometres Sprint - 85th * 20 kilometres - 85th ==== Cross-country skiing ==== * Men's 50 kilometres - DNF ==References== Category:1967 births Category:Living people Category:Serbian male biathletes Category:Serbian male cyclists Category:Serbian male cross-country skiers Category:Olympic cross- country skiers for Yugoslavia Category:Olympic cyclists as Independent Olympic Participants Category:Olympic biathletes for Serbia and Montenegro Category:Olympic cross-country skiers for Serbia and Montenegro Category:Cross-country skiers at the 1992 Winter Olympics Category:Cyclists at the 1992 Summer Olympics Category:Biathletes at the 2006 Winter Olympics Category:Cross-country skiers at the 2006 Winter Olympics Category:Sportspeople from Belgrade Category:Serbia and Montenegro sportsmen
