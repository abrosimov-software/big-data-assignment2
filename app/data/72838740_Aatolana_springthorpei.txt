Aatolana springthorpei is a species of crustaceans in the family Cirolanidae, first described by Stephen John Keable in 1998. It is benthic shrimp found in tropical waters at depths of 200 m to 203 m on the continental shelves of Queensland and New South Wales. Like other species in this genus it is a scavenger.
