Atlanta Fashion Week (ATLFW) is an annual fashion week in Atlanta, Georgia and supported by the mayors office as the "official" fashion week of Atlanta. The event includes press conferences, fashion shows, and designer meetups. == History == Between 2013 and 2018 the event was held under the name; Ragtrade Atlanta Fashion Week. In 2019 the event rebranded into Atlanta Fashion Week and announced official support from the City of Atlanta Mayor’s Office of Film and Entertainment as well as Invest Atlanta. At the press conference it was announced the four day event would take place August 5 – 9, 2020, but was delayed due to the COVID-19 pandemic. The event was held under the new branding for the first time in October 5 - 8, 2022.
