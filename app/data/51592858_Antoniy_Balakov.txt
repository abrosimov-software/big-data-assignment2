Antoniy Balakov (Bulgarian: Антоний Балъков; born 14 December 1988) is a Bulgarian footballer who plays as a midfielder. He is a nephew of former footballer Krasimir Balakov. ==Career== On 20 January 2017, Balakov joined Litex Lovech. He helped the team to achieve promotion to Second League but was released in June. In July 2017, he returned to Lokomotiv Gorna Oryahovitsa.
