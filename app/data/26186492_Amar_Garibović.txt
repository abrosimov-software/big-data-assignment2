Amar Garibović (; 7 September 1991 – 7 September 2010) was a Serbian cross- country skier who had competed since 2004. He finished 80th in the 15 km event at the 2010 Winter Olympics in Vancouver. Garibović finished 109th in the individual sprint at the FIS Nordic World Ski Championships 2009 in Liberec. His lone win was at a 10 km event in Bosnia and Herzegovina in 2009. Garibović died in a car crash in Croatia on 7 September 2010, the day of his 19th birthday.
