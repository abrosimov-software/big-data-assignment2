Ambar () is a village in the Bismil District of Diyarbakır Province in Turkey. The village is populated by Muhacir Bulgarians and Kurds and had a population of 1,937 in 2022. == History == Bulgarian refugees were settled in the village by the authorities to Turkify the region, however the settlers would become integrated into the surrounding Kurdish culture. == References == Category:Villages in Bismil District Category:Kurdish settlements in Diyarbakır Province
