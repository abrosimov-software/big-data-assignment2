The Agly (; ; ) is a river located in southern France.With a length of 79.9 km (49.6 mi), it originates in the hills of the Corbières Massif near Camps- sur-l'Agly. The river passes through various towns and villages including Saint-Paul-de-Fenouillet, Estagel, Rivesaltes and Saint-Laurent-de-la-Salanque before it flows into the Mediterranean Sea near Le Barcarès. ==Tributaries== * Boulzane * Désix * Verdouble ==References== Category:Rivers of France Category:Rivers of Aude Category:Rivers of Pyrénées-Orientales Category:Rivers of Occitania (administrative region) 0Agly
