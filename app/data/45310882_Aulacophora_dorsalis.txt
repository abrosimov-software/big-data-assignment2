Aulacophora dorsalis is a species of beetles in the family Chrysomelidae. ==Description== Aulacophora dorsalis can reach a length of about . The basic color of these beetles is yellowish, with two black transversal bars at the top and bottom of the elytra. ==Distribution== This species is native to Southeast Asia. ==References== D Category:Beetles described in 1835 Category:Beetles of Asia
