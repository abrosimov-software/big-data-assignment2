Abrashino () is a rural locality (a village) in Nizhnekamensky Selsoviet of Ordynsky District, Novosibirsk Oblast, Russia. The population was 37 as of 2010. There are 6 streets. == Geography == Abrashino is located 57 km southwest of Ordynskoye (the district's administrative centre) by road.
