Andrzej Tadeusz Mazurkiewicz (born 11 May 1963 in Jarosław; died 21 March 2008 in Warsaw) was a Polish politician. He was an MP for the first independent government between 1991 and 1993, and later a three-time senator (1997–2001 and 2005–2008).
