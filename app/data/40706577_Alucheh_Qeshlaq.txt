Alucheh Qeshlaq (, also Romanized as Ālūcheh Qeshlāq; also known as Alcheh Gheshlagh, Ālcheh Qeshlāq, Kyshlag, Qeshlāq, Qeshlāq Ālūcheh, and Qishlāq) is a village in Bakrabad Rural District, in the Central District of Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 310, in 67 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
